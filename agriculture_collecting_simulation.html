Challenge

Digitizing the Field

This project aims to develop a VR training system that transports agricultural workforce training into a digital environment. Specifically targeting the strawberry picking process, it enables workers to learn correct picking techniques and improve their application skills in a realistic virtual setting.

Training Approach

Train Like You Work

01

Realistic Environment

Users experience 1:1 real-world field conditions. Every row, leaf, and strawberry is modeled to replicate the actual working environment.

02

Interactive Feedback

The application provides instant feedback. Picked a green strawberry? Applied too much pressure? The system alerts you immediately.

03

Risk-Free Learning

Make mistakes here, not in the harvest. Workers can refine their skills without damaging actual crops or wasting valuable resources.

Unique Approach

Simulating Precision

What sets this project apart is its focus on agriculture-specific manual processes rather than generic skills. We simulate the exact picking motion using advanced hand technology.

Scalable Architecture: This system isn't limited to strawberries. Its flexible structure can be adapted for any crop or agricultural scenario.

Waste Reduction: By training virtually, product waste during the learning phase is eliminated.

Immersive Control

Precision in Your Hands

Picking a strawberry requires more than just a button press. It demands delicacy, the right angle, and precise pressure. Our advanced Hand Tracking system eliminates the need for controllers, allowing users to use their natural hand movements.
